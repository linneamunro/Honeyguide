The Honeyguide's Revenge


In this story "The Honeyguide's Revenge," Gingile gets honeycomb out of a hive. He uses a smoking stick to calm the bees first. How does the smoking stick work? When there is smoke the bees cannot detect alarm signals that would normally cause them to attack an intruder. Smoke also makes them think there is a forest fire so they eat a lot of honey before leaving their hive to look for a new home. It is hard for them to sting when they are full of honey.
